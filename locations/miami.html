Forensic Engineering in Miami & Southeast Florida

Miami's unique coastal environment presents distinct challenges for structural engineering. From high-rise condominium recertification requirements to hurricane damage assessment, our licensed professional engineers understand the specific needs of Southeast Florida properties.

Following the Surfside tragedy and the passage of Senate Bill 4-D, Florida condominiums face new milestone inspection requirements. We help HOAs, property managers, and building owners navigate these critical structural integrity requirements.
