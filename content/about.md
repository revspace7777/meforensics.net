# About Mertsching Enterprises (ME Forensics)

## Our Mission

**"It Is Not About ME, It Is About YOU"**

At Mertsching Enterprises, LLC, our mission is to provide impartial, scientifically sound, and timely engineering services. We exist to bring clarity to complex structural problems, allowing property owners, insurers, and attorneys to make informed decisions based on hard data, not speculation.

As a certified **Woman-Owned Business Enterprise (WBE)**, we are committed to excellence, integrity, and the advancement of diverse leadership in the engineering field. We understand that behind every claim or inspection is a person or business needing a resolution, and we strive to provide that resolution with both technical rigor and professional empathy.

## Leadership

### Theresa Mertsching, P.E.
**Principal Engineer & President**

Theresa Mertsching is a Licensed Professional Engineer with extensive experience in forensic engineering, structural design, and construction management. Licensed in Florida, Georgia, Texas, and additional states, she brings a multi-disciplinary approach to forensic investigation.

Theresa's expertise spans:
- **Forensic Investigation:** Cause and origin analysis of structural failures, water intrusion, and building envelope defects.
- **Disaster Response:** Rapid assessment of hurricane, tornado, and flood damage.
- **Expert Testimony:** Providing deposition and trial testimony that simplifies complex engineering concepts for judges and juries.
- **Regulatory Compliance:** Deep knowledge of the Florida Building Code, Condo Safety Act (SB 4D/SB 154), and municipal ordinances.

## Our Capabilities

We employ a "boots on the ground" approach combined with advanced diagnostic technology:

*   **Aerial & Underwater Drones:** FAA Part 107 certified pilots for roof and tower inspections; ROVs for seawall and bridge scour evaluations.
*   **Non-Destructive Testing (NDT):** Thermal imaging, moisture mapping, and sounding to detect hidden defects without destructive intrusion.
*   **Litigation Support:** Comprehensive file review, peer review of opposing reports, and expert witness services.
