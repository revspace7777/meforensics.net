# Expert Witness & Litigation Support

## Turning Complexity into Clarity

In legal disputes involving structural failures or construction defects, the ability to explain complex engineering concepts to a judge or jury is paramount. One can effectively bridge the gap between technical data and legal arguments.

## Our Qualifications

*   **Licensed Professional Engineers:** Registered in Florida and multiple other states.
*   **Daubert-Qualified:** Experience meeting the rigorous standards for expert testimony.
*   **Trial Experience:** Proven track record in depositions, mediations, and courtroom testimony.

## Litigation Support Services

### 1. File Review & Peer Review
We provide objective reviews of opposing expert reports to identify technical flaws, code misinterpretations, or unsupported conclusions.

### 2. Deposition & Trial Testimony
We prepare clear, visual, and fact-based testimony that withstands cross-examination. Our focus is always on the scientific truth of the matter.

### 3. Mediation Support
We assist counsel/adjusters during mediation by providing real-time technical advice to help resolve disputes before they reach trial.

## Areas of Expertise

*   **Construction Defects:** 558 Claims, stucco failures, window installation errors.
*   **Structural Failures:** Collapse, partial collapse, and component failure.
*   **Code Compliance:** Analysis of violations of the Florida Building Code.
*   **Premises Liability:** Slip and fall assessments related to code-compliant walking surfaces (trip hazards).
