## The "Florida Horseshoe" Strategy

We strategically serve high-density coastal regions where the intersection of aging infrastructure, extreme weather, and complex insurance claims demands specialized expertise.

- **Miami & South Florida:** Condo Milestone Inspections, Concrete Restoration, Sea Level Resilience.
- **Orlando & Central Florida:** Commercial Development, Theme Park Structures, Sinkhole Investigation.
- **Tampa Bay:** Wind vs. Flood determinations, Roof Damage Assessments, Marine Structure Evaluations.
- **Southwest Florida:** Post-Hurricane Disaster Response, Reconstruction Monitoring.
