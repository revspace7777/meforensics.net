Forensic Engineering Services

We investigate structural failures, determine root causes, and provide expert analysis for insurance claims, litigation, and building rehabilitation.

What is Forensic Engineering?

Forensic engineering is the investigation of materials, products, structures, or components that fail or do not operate as intended. Our licensed professional engineers apply scientific methods to determine the root cause of failures and provide expert opinions for legal proceedings.

Whether you're dealing with a collapsed structure, water intrusion damage, fire-related deterioration, or vehicle impact damage, our team provides thorough investigations and detailed engineering reports that stand up in court.

Our Forensic Engineering Specialties

Structural Failure Analysis

Complete investigation of collapsed or damaged structures including load path analysis, material testing, and failure mode identification.

Origin & Cause Investigation

Systematic investigation to determine the origin point and underlying cause of structural damage, water intrusion, or building envelope failures.

Fire Damage Assessment

Post-fire structural evaluation to determine the extent of damage, remaining structural integrity, and required repairs or demolition.

Vehicle Impact Analysis

Assessment of structural damage caused by vehicle impacts, including load redistribution analysis and repair recommendations.

Our Process

  1. 1

    Site Investigation

    Comprehensive on-site inspection with photographic documentation, measurements, and sample collection as needed.

  2. 2

    Document Review

    Analysis of building plans, maintenance records, weather data, and any prior inspection reports.

  3. 3

    Engineering Analysis

    Application of engineering principles to determine failure mechanisms and contributing factors.

  4. 4

    Expert Report

    Detailed written report with findings, conclusions, and recommendations suitable for litigation or insurance claims.
