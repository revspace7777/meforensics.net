Our Story

Mertsching Enterprises, LLC was founded with a simple mission: to provide honest, thorough, and client-focused forensic engineering services to Florida property owners, attorneys, insurance companies, and HOAs.

As a Women Owned Business Enterprise (WBE), we understand that behind every damaged building is a family, a business, or a community in need. Our approach combines technical excellence with genuine empathy—we're not just engineers, we're problem solvers who care about outcomes.

From our headquarters in New Port Richey, we serve the entire "Florida Horseshoe" corridor—from Miami's high-rise condominiums to Tampa Bay's waterfront properties to Naples' luxury estates. We respond when disasters strike and stand ready to support our clients through every phase of investigation, litigation, and recovery.
